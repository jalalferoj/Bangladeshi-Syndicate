Who We Are: The Syndicate Core

Founded by a group of passionate young Bangladeshi-Australians, the **Bangladeshi Syndicate** started with a simple goal: to create a home away from home in Melbourne. We saw a need for modern, engaging, and professional events that celebrate our rich culture while integrating seamlessly into the vibrant Australian landscape.

We are not just event organizers; we are **community architects**. Every event, from a small 'Adda' to a major festival, is designed to strengthen ties, support new arrivals, and keep the spirit of Bangladesh alive for the younger generation.
